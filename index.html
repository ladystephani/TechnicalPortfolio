About Me

Hi! I’m a coding bootcamp student at Columbia Engineering. Welcome to my page!

A few fun facts about me: With Hakka Chinese roots, I am currently residing in metro Vancouver in the Pacific Northwest. I’m a recent Johns Hopkins alum, and my ties with Columbia started when I did a Columbia Global Centers exchange program in Paris back then. Starting out college in the pre-med path but later shifted towards the arts and sciences, I have a background in pure mathematics and German and Roman languages with a focus on French literature. I’m passionate about technology, especially digital healthcare, and have work experience with the UN, organizing a UNOPS international health summit in 2019.

As of right now, I’m working on building my toolkit in full-stack web development. Please look forward to more of my upcoming projects.
